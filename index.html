Data Analysis w/ Excel

This is an excel exercise to draw insights and answer shareholder questions given a large quantity of raw data, which pertained to sales volume made by a firm. First, the data was organized by various categories and displayed in data tables. Insights such as growth rates were calculated. The final sheet displays the finished product that will be presented to shareholders, which presents the information and insights in a clear, concise, and visually pleasing manner.
